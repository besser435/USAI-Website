How it works

Twice per month, USAI will sell lottery tickets. Each player can buy as many tickets as they wish. The jackpot is funded by ticket sales, and will occasionally have a bonus that USAI adds.

What is the USAI Bonus?

The USAI Bonus is additional money added to the jackpot by USAI at no cost to ticket holders. This is to incentivize people to buy tickets due to the larger jackpot.

How much of the jackpot does the winner get?

The winner is entitled to no less than 85% of the jackpot. The remaining funds are reserved for USAI to use as we see fit. They will mainly to expand the jackpot for future drawings, or for other USAI projects that benefit the public.
